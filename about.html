About us

We are dedicated to supporting different research
on drug use and addiction.For over 10 years, we have
have been advancing the science on drug use and
addiction and public health.

Our mission

Our mission is to support and conduct basic,
,clinical and social research on drug abuse,
its neurobiological,behavioural and social mechanisims
involved.
We aim to ensure the effective tranlation,implementation
and dissemination of scientific research findings to improve
the prevention and treatment of substance use disorders (SUDs)
reduce the harms associated with drug use,guide policies,
enhance public awareness of addiction as a chronic but
treatable medical illness, and reduce stigma


Our Goals

This are some of what we try to achieve:

Identify and develop approaches to reduce stigma

Identify and develop approaches to reduce drug abuse

Develop personalized interventions informed by
people with lived experience.
